Fakelandia's Local Aquarium started 5 years ago seeking to be the best source of local information in regards to local marine flora and fauna. We make sure to contact global and in-house experts in the field to ensure that our residents have the best quality habitats.
In addition to spreading accurate information to young and old visitors, we also do our own fair share of Research and Discovery (R&D) by running and funding our own projects with your contributions. Any discoveries we make are made sure to be appropriately disclosed to the scientific community.
