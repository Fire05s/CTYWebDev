Website Structure

Page Title Content Planned Location/Path
Homepage Brief summary about the aquarium, its purpose, pictures, any relevant links, etc. Found on first opening and can be re-found by clicking the logo/"Home" tab on the toolbar.
About Us History of the aquarium, more in-depth talk about its purpose, pictures of the team, etc. Found through the "About Us" tab on the toolbar or linked by the Homepage.
Conservation Efforts Current active teams, effects of efforts, and advice for those who are interested in getting involved. Found through the "Conservation Efforts" tab on the toolbar.
Donate Tracking of where the money goes, restatement of purpose, and pictures of effects. Found through the "Donate" tab on the toolbar or linked by the Homepage.
Research Past - show pictures of past projects, teams, and results; Present - show ongoing project pictures, teams, and purposes Found through the "Research" tab on the toolbar with a dropdown menu showing Past or Present.
Tickets Map of the aquarium and option to give information to sign up and pay. Found through the "Tickets" tab on the toolbar or linked by the Homepage.
Join Us! Why they should join the team, an application form allowing for the receiving of information, and any following steps. Found through the "Join Us!" tab on the toolbar.
